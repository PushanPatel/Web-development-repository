Alleppey, Kerala


Back in the 20th Century the Viceroy of the Indian Empire,
 Lord Curzon called Alleppey, the Venice of the East and rightly so. 
Located in Gods own Country, Kerela, Alleppey is one of the 
most heavenly places in India. Alleppey is known as Alappuzha 
by the local people .Closest to nature and stunningly beautiful.
Best time to visit – June to September
