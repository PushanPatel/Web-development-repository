Orchha, Madhya Pradesh

Orchha, founded by, Rudra Pratap, is known for the exquisite 
Jehangir Mahal, a tiered palace. The view of soaring temple spires 
and cenotaphs from the palace is spectacular. The vibrant murals 
in the Laxminarayan Temple and Raj Mahal, encompasses a variety 
of religious and secular themes, bringing the walls and ceilings to life. 
The Stern, the shrines and the temples in the vicinity add colors to the 
poignant history of Orchha.
Best time to visit – September to March
